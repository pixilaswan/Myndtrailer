About MyndTrail

MyndTrail connects meditation teachers and students, fostering better communication and guidance. It is an innovative platform designed to connect users with meditation coaches for personalized mental wellness support. The platform offers guided meditation sessions, customized journaling, and AI-powered recommendations, all tailored to the unique needs of each user. Coaches can create and publish curated meditation curriculums, track students' progress, and offer personalized guidance based on individual experiences
